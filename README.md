### 👨‍🔬 About Me

I'm a **Chemical Engineering** student at **BITS Pilani, Goa Campus** who loves bridging the gap between hardcore engineering mathematics and beautiful software. When I'm not studying fluid dynamics, I'm probably ricing my Linux desktop or building high-performance CLI tools. 

- 💻 **Current Focus:** Wayland desktop customization (especially Niri & Noctalia) and writing blazingly fast Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) simulations.
- 🔬 **Engineering:** Deeply interested in transport phenomena, numerical methods, and OpenFOAM.
- 🎵 **Side Quests:** I build cool plugins, like a zero-config time-synced [Spotify Lyrics Plugin](https://github.com/goatnath/community-plugins) for the Noctalia shell.
